Experience


Advertising & Design

2010-2016 TBWA\Chiat, New York, NY

Clients: Absolut, Accenture, Bein Sports, BNY Mellon, Coopervision, Ethicon, Hewlett-Packard, Jameson, Kahlua, Michelin, Nissan, Planters, SAP. Position involved page layout, production and design. Position encompassed work for all above clients with an emphasis on Ethicon, a medical supply company. Design and production of ads, brochures, convention materials, web banners and email blasts. Results: campaigns received numerous awards and professional recognition, medical community has increased awareness of Ethicon supplies and conventions.

1999-2010 Dentsu America, New York, NY

Clients: 180’s Sportswear, BusinessWeek, Canon, HarperCollins Publishers, J.A. Henckels, JVC Jazz Festival, Philips, Toyota, Uniqlo. Worked with creative team in producing print and designing ad campaigns, inserts, outdoor advertising, direct mail, packaging and web banners. Results: Effie Awards for sales generated (BusinessWeek), numerous awards for outstanding design and printing (JVC Jazz Festival), increased sales and awareness of new book promotions (HarperCollins). Helped in campaigns to acquire new business for 180’s Sportswear, Toyota Global and Philips. Ad campaigns established Canon as a leader in digital technology, increased awareness of JVC jazz festival. Worked on creative developement and ad campaigns for Uniqlo products. Ads, billboards, mailers, product books, wild postings and subway car postings established Uniqlo as a brand leader in the U.S. market.

2000-2002 Tenga Studio, New York, NY

Clients: Philip Morris Company. Position involved producing from concept to finished piece internal brochures, postings, and newsletters for Philip Morris Company employees nation wide. Results: increased employee awareness of company’s core values and mission.

1997-1999 SteinRogan + Partners, New York, NY

Clients: Apartments.com, Auctions.com, Cars.com, Homehunter.com, FastTV, Hotmail.com, Meredith Publishing, New York Times, Schmid, SmartMoney, Snowball.com, The Stock Market, SundanceChannel. Worked with creative teams designing and producing ads, brochures, newsletters and direct mail. Results: campaigns received numerous awards and professional recognition.
